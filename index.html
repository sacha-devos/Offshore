Essec Offshore

Essec Offshore is a vibrant student association at ESSEC Business School, focused on water sports such as sailing, windsurfing, and kite surfing. Welcoming students of all skill levels, the group offers training, competitive events, and social activities, promoting teamwork and passion for the nautical lifestyle.
